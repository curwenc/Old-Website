ABOUT ME

The Photographer: Carl Curwen

Twenty-three. Born and raised in Brooklyn. Picked up a refurbished Nikon D40X in 2007 and it's been love ever since. Constantly traveling the depths of New York, from dingy to glitzy, to photograph.


You're not Pablo Picasso. What gives?

Obviously not. It was a nickname given to me by some overly supportive friends when I was first starting out and didn't know what or who an ISO was. I let the nickname stick because there was no greater motivation to become as good as artist (albeit thru different mediums) as he was. As time has passed, I have been fortunate to learn what ISO is; as well as improve. There is no benchmark in determining what is a great photographer is. However, like Pablo Picasso, people were able to invoke/experience emotion and thought from his art. I only hope to acheive what he did by using the best city in the world, New York City, to help you think/grow. I am in no way related to the man but I am proud to say I am New York City's Picasso.
